## Topics Covered

This test assesses:
1. **Fundamental Chemistry** - Bonding, hybridization, electronic structure
2. **Nanomaterial Properties** - Quantum confinement, size effects, dimensionality
3. **Synthesis Methods** - Top-down vs bottom-up approaches
4. **Material Structure** - Crystal structure, grain boundaries, mechanical properties
5. **Characterization** - XRD, microscopy, spectroscopy
6. **Calculations** - Surface area to volume ratios, elastic modulus, stress-strain
7. **Applications** - Real-world uses of nanomaterials

**Test Design Philosophy:**
- Minimizes pure memorization/binder recall
- Tests conceptual understanding
- Includes calculation skills at various difficulty levels
- Covers breadth of materials science topics
- Time-efficient for 30-minute format
- Mix of multiple choice and free response questions
