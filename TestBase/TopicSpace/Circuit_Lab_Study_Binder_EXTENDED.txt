--- DIAGRAM 16: CIRCUIT ANALYSIS DECISION FLOWCHART ---
Type: Flowchart with decision points
Content:
  - Start: "Understand the circuit"
  - Decision 1: "Series or Parallel?"
    - Series path: Use R_eq = ΣR
    - Parallel path: Use 1/R_eq = Σ(1/R)
  - Decision 2: "Mixed? Yes→ Reduce step by step"
  - Decision 3: "What are we solving for?"
    - Voltage: Use V = IR
    - Current: Use I = V/R
    - Resistance: Use R = V/I
  - Decision 4: "AC or DC?"
    - DC: Use resistive calculations
    - AC: Use impedance Z = R + jX
  - End: "Verify with power balance"
  
Use: Circuit analysis strategy section (4.1)

--- DIAGRAM 20: LED SERIES RESISTOR CALCULATION EXAMPLE ---
Type: Step-by-step calculation visual
Content:
  - Circuit diagram: 9V battery → Resistor R → Red LED → Ground
  - Given:
    - V_supply = 9V
    - V_LED (red) = 1.8V
    - I_LED (desired) = 20mA
  - Step 1: V_resistor = V_supply - V_LED = 9 - 1.8 = 7.2V
  - Step 2: R = V_resistor / I = 7.2 / 0.020 = 360Ω
  - Step 3: Select 360Ω or 390Ω standard resistor
  - Step 4: Verify power: P = 7.2V × 0.020A = 144mW (use ≥1/4W resistor)
  - Show color codes for 360Ω (Orange-Blue-Brown-Gold)
  
Use: LED section (2.4) or circuit design examples

INTEGRATION NOTES FOR DOCUMENT CREATOR
================================================================================

These diagrams should be:
1. High contrast for readability
2. Labeled clearly with values and units
3. Color-coded consistently (Red=positive/wire, Blue=current, Green=resistive)
4. Sized appropriately (can be printed on page margins or full page)
5. Referenced in text with exact location noted "[ADD IMAGE: Description]"
6. Saved as high-quality images (PNG or PDF, at least 300 DPI for printing)

Priority ranking for creation (do these first):
  HIGH: Diagrams 1, 2, 3 (Power, Series/Parallel circuits)
  HIGH: Diagrams 11, 12 (RC curves - critical for understanding)
  HIGH: Diagrams 5, 6 (Component specs - frequently referenced)
  MEDIUM: Diagrams 8, 9, 13 (Magnetism/Right-hand rule)
  MEDIUM: Diagrams 4, 7 (Component details)
  MEDIUM: Diagrams 14, 15 (Comparisons and transformers)
  LOW: Diagrams 10 (AC waveform)
  REFERENCE: Diagrams 16, 17, 18, 19, 20 (Flowcharts and procedures)

Software recommendations for creating diagrams:
- Circuit diagrams: LTspice, Circuit Lab, Draw.io, Inkscape
- Graphs/charts: Matplotlib (Python), Excel, Desmos
- Vector graphics: Inkscape, Adobe Illustrator
- Hand diagrams: Procreate, Adobe Fresco, or hand-drawn then scanned
- Flowcharts: Lucidchart, Draw.io, Miro
