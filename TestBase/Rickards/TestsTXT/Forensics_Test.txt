It was a cold October night at Nevermore Academy, and the annual Rave‚ÄôN Dance was in full 
swing. Students were dressed in their best attire, enjoying the eerie decorations and

next to the spilled punch bowl that tested positive for Ricksane-ium (C20H25N3O).
