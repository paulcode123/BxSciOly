---CRIME SCENE BACKGROUND---
A cold October night at Nevermore Academy during the annual Rave'N Dance. At 10:42 pm, lights went out for no longer than a minute. When lights returned, Principal Larissa Weems was found collapsed near the refreshment table. A half-eaten cupcake sat nearby, next to a spilled punch bowl that tested positive for Ricksane-ium (C20H25N3O). Evidence was collected from the Academy Kitchen and Refreshments Table. Under a fridge in the Academy Kitchen, a plastic baggie with sticky-note with "Ricksane-ium" sloppily-written on it was found. On the plastic baggie, investigators found fingerprints, traces of DNA, and traces of an unknown chemical analyzed via mass spectrometry. 5 students have been brought in as prime suspects.
