About the House Cup

The House Cup is a yearlong competition in Science Olympiad where all admitted members are assigned to one of 4 houses.

  • All points earned in activities and competitions during event meetings go to your house
  • A trophy is awarded at the end of the year to the winning house
  • Individual awards are given to top performers
  • Purpose: Make meetings collaborative, exciting, and worth coming to
  • Goals: Foster teamwork, develop competition skills, and motivate self-study
