Earth and Space Science

Events from Science Olympiad

Earth and Space Science events in Science Olympiad cover a wide range of topics from astronomy to geology. These events challenge students to understand the processes that shape our planet and the cosmic context in which it exists.

2025 Earth and Space Science Events

Astronomy

Students will demonstrate an understanding of stellar evolution and variable stars through analysis, calculation, and graphical interpretation of provided data.

Study Event Team of 2

Dynamic Planet

Students will use process skills to complete tasks related to Earth's fresh waters, including physical and ecological processes, human impacts, and conservation.

Study Event Team of 2

Fossils

Students will demonstrate their knowledge of ancient life by identifying fossils and their classifications, as well as answering questions on preservation, evolution, and paleoecology.

Study Event Team of 2

Geologic Mapping

Students will demonstrate their understanding of topographic maps, geologic maps, structural geology, and geological tools and techniques.
