SciWeb Initiative

You may be wondering, who am I to make these changes? Well, 2 years ago, I founded the SciWeb Initiative, which has evolved into a platform where people can learn just about anything incredibly quickly through concept map architecture paired with State-space AI.

Learn Using Concept Maps

Click on concepts to see connections:

In short, what we figured out is that the human brain is a state machine, storing information not in sentences, but in a network of interconnected concepts.

When we use AI tools to transcribe the user's speech and ideas and integrate them into this map, we can then directly challenge and probe the user's knowledge.

I plan to deploy this technology to Science Olympiad, boosting our team's ability to learn and retain advanced material.
