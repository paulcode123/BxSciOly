Events from Science Olympiad

Chemistry events in Science Olympiad focus on understanding the fundamental aspects of matter and chemical reactions. These events combine theoretical knowledge with practical laboratory skills to solve complex problems.

2025 Chemistry Events

Chem Lab

Students will demonstrate knowledge of chemistry principles related to aqueous solutions, chemical kinetics, and oxidation-reduction reactions through theoretical and lab-based problems.

Lab Event Team of 2

Forensics

Students will identify substances by using physical and chemical properties to complete a series of tests and solve a crime. Topics include analysis of powders, polymers, fibers, fingerprints, DNA, and evidence collection.

Lab Event Team of 2

Materials Science

Students will use their understanding of material properties and characteristics to identify, evaluate, and solve problems related to materials science.

Study/Lab Event Team of 2

Optics

Students will demonstrate their knowledge of geometric and physical optics through problem-solving and hands-on activities involving optical instruments and devices.
