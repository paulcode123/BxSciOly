What is Science Olympiad?

Science Olympiad is a team competition where students compete in events across various STEM disciplines, from Astronomy to Engineering. At Bronx Science, our team combines academic knowledge with hands-on building skills to excel in regional, state, and national competitions.

Explore Our Events

Benefits of joining the team

  • Strong community, get to go to competitions together
  • You'll learn how to understand and apply knowledge more comprehensively and efficiently
  • You get to explore your interests across a wide range of topics

What we do

  • We compete in number of competitions each year, including several in-person and virtual invitationals, regionals, and states
  • Each year there are 18 academic events, ranging from Astronomy to Microbe Mission to Materials Science, and everything in between
  • Academic events, such as Anatomy and Physiology and Codebusters, involve taking open-notes tests that require familiarity with a wide range of material
  • Each event meets once a week, and these meetings are largely focused on team bonding and having fun

How to join

  • Choose 2-5 events you're interested in, and get started on studying them on our study page! If you start well in advance, you're exponentially more likely to make the team!
  • Attend tryouts, which are held around October. These will assess the exact material you've studied, so make sure you're well prepared!

Benefits of joining the team

  • You get to work on building cool stuff with your friends
  • Gain valuable skills in math, engineering, and design
  • You'll work closely with your teammates to complete a larger project

What we do

  • We compete in number of competitions each year, including several in-person and virtual invitationals, regionals, and states
  • Each year there are 5 build events, exploring a wide range of engineering and physics topics
  • Build events, such as Bungee Drop and Air Trajectory, involve building physical structures to precise specifications
  • You'll come in multiple times a week to work on the builds with your team

How to join

  • Choose 1-3 build events you're interested in, and get started on building and testing your own models! You can track your accuracy and progress on our build page
  • Attend tryouts, which are held around October. You'll showcase your model, and we'll assess your dedication and skills
