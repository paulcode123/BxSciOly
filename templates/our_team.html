Our Team

Building a legacy of excellence in science competition

Our History

The Bronx High School of Science has a long and proud tradition of excellence in STEM education. Our Science Olympiad team was established circa 2010 as a small academic team.What began as a small team gradually grew into one of the school's most successful academic teams. Over the years, we've built a reputation of excellence and merit in the New York Science Olympiad circuit, consistently placing among the top teams in regional competitions and qualifying at the state level. Our rigorous preparation, dedicated advisors, and passionate students have helped establish Bronx Science as a powerhouse in science competitions.

Today, our team consists of over 150 active members across three competitive teams (A, B, and C), with students specializing in various events ranging from biology and chemistry to physics, earth science, and engineering. Our exceptional growth reflects our commitment to fostering the next generation of scientists and cultivating teamwork among our student body.

Regional Dominance

The Bronx Science Olympiad team has established itself as a dominant force in the New York City regional competitions. For the past decade, we have consistently placed multiple teams in the top 5 rankings, demonstrating our specific preparation and commitment to success in science.

Our success stems from our approach to team structure and preparation. We send three competitive teams—A, B, and C—each composed of 15 students who compete across 23 events. This structure is uniquely supported by our Event Manager system, which allows students to support each other in the learning journey to prepare students for competitions and invitationals throughout the United States.

In regional competitions, our A team has secured first place for five consecutive years, while our B and C teams regularly outperform most schools' primary teams. This consistent excellence across all three teams demonstrates the exceptional quality of our science education and the unique passion of our students to succeed.

State Competitions & National Aspirations

While we've consistently demonstrated our excellence at the regional level, our sights are set on greater achievements. At the New York State Science Olympiad Competition, we compete against the best teams from across the state, and have shown steady improvement over the years, climbing from 40th place in 2018 to 11th in 2021 and 12th in 2024 and 2026.

Our ultimate goal is to represent New York at the National Science Olympiad Tournament. We recognize that reaching this level requires extraordinary dedication, preparation, and performance. Each year, we refine our training methods, strengthen our knowledge base, and enhance our build designs to move closer to this objective.

For the 2025 season, we have dedicated more time in refining these skills through a plethora of resources to help us break into the top 10 at the state level. With continued determination and hard work, we believe our goal of reaching the national level is within reach in the coming years.
