CASE #001 GEOLOGICAL
🏔️

Rocks & Minerals Investigation

EXPERT LEVEL

Conduct comprehensive geological surveys. Master the identification of complex mineral specimens and understand the formation processes that create Earth's diverse rock types.

🛠️ Investigation Tools:

📖 Unlimited Reference Binder
🔍 Physical Property Tests
⚖️ Hardness & Density Analysis

Core Topics

  • Mineral properties: hardness, luster, streak, cleavage
  • Rock types and formation environments
  • Economic uses and diagnostic features

Practice Plan

  • Timed ID drills using property-first decision trees
  • Maintain annotated specimen photos and tables

Helpful Tools

  • Streak plate, hand lens, magnet, hardness picks (if allowed)

🎯 Hands-On Training:

💎 Mineral Identification Workshop

Learn to identify over 50 common minerals using streak, hardness, luster, and crystal form. Practice with real specimens in our geology lab.

Visual Analysis Physical Testing Systematic Classification
🌋 Rock Formation Analysis

Understand igneous, sedimentary, and metamorphic processes. Create detailed formation timelines and environmental reconstructions.

Process Analysis Timeline Construction Environmental Reasoning
🗺️ Field Investigation Techniques

Master field identification methods, geological mapping, and sample collection protocols used by professional geologists.

Field Methods Mapping Skills Professional Protocols
CASE #002 BIOLOGICAL
🦗

Entomology Investigation

ADVANCED LEVEL

Become a master insect detective. Learn to identify North American insect families through detailed morphological analysis and ecological understanding.

🛠️ Investigation Tools:

📚 Comprehensive Family Guide
🔬 Morphological Analysis
🌿 Ecological Context Clues

Core Topics

  • Orders/families; diagnostic traits and anatomy vocabulary
  • Life cycles, metamorphosis, and ecological roles

Practice Plan

  • Flashcard diagnostic characters; labeled anatomy drills
  • Binder tabs with side-by-side family comparisons

🎯 Hands-On Training:

🔬 Insect Anatomy Mastery

Dissect and analyze insect morphology. Learn to distinguish families by wing venation, antennae structure, and body segmentation.

Anatomical Analysis Microscopic Detail Comparative Morphology
🌱 Behavior & Ecology Study

Connect insect identification with ecological roles, life cycles, and behavioral patterns. Understand insect-environment interactions.

Ecological Reasoning Life Cycle Analysis Behavioral Observation
CASE #003 ASTRONOMICAL
🌟

Stellar Evolution Investigation

MASTER LEVEL

Investigate the lifecycle of stars and hunt for exoplanets. Master stellar classification systems and understand the complex processes that govern cosmic evolution.

🛠️ Investigation Tools:

🔭 Stellar Classification Charts
📊 H-R Diagram Analysis
🪐 Exoplanet Detection Data

Core Topics

  • Stellar evolution, HR diagram, blackbody radiation
  • Exoplanet detection: transit and radial velocity

Practice Plan

  • Past tests; build formula sheet (distance modulus, luminosity)
  • Image/spectrum interpretation drills

🎯 Hands-On Training:

⭐ Stellar Classification System

Master the OBAFGKM system and place stars on H-R diagrams. Analyze spectral data to determine temperature, luminosity, and evolutionary stage.

Spectral Analysis Data Interpretation Classification Systems
🪐 Exoplanet Detection Methods

Learn transit photometry, radial velocity methods, and gravitational microlensing. Analyze real astronomical data to discover new worlds.

Data Analysis Detection Methods Research Techniques
