🌍 Real-World Impact

From designing spacecraft components to creating medical devices, CAD skills open doors to every engineering field.

Key Skills

  • Parametric modeling and fully constrained sketches
  • Assembly mates and motion; design intent and revisions
  • Reading and reproducing engineering drawings and GD&T basics

Core Topics

  • Dimensioning standards, tolerances, section and detail views
  • BOMs, exploded views, subassemblies vs assemblies

Practice Plan

  • Speed-model drills from provided drawings
  • Weekly assembly challenges with specified mates
  • Create drawings for peer review; iterate with feedback

Machines

Build lever-based measuring devices and master the principles of simple and compound machines. Learn how mechanical advantage multiplies human capability.

🌍 Real-World Impact

Every crane, car jack, and robotics system uses these principles. Master machines, master the mechanical world.

Key Skills

  • Mechanical advantage analysis (IMA vs AMA) and efficiency
  • Lever device construction and calibration for measurement
  • Quick, accurate calculations under time pressure

Core Topics

  • Levers, pulleys, gears, wheel-and-axle; torque and moments
  • Error analysis and uncertainty in device readings

Practice Plan

  • Lab verification of MA/efficiency across devices
  • Calibrate and produce curves for your lever-based device
  • Timed problem sets mixing theory and device use

Circuit Lab

Electrify Your Understanding

Build circuits, analyze electrical behavior, and explore electromagnetic phenomena. From Ohm's law to complex AC circuits, master the language of electricity.

⚡ Circuit Analysis
🔬 Lab Techniques
📊 Electrical Measurements

🎮 Build Your First Circuit

Click components above to build a simple LED circuit!

🌍 Real-World Impact

From smartphones to electric cars, everything runs on circuits. Master electrons, master technology.

Key Skills

  • DC circuit analysis; Thevenin/Norton reductions
  • Using multimeters/power supplies safely and effectively
  • Troubleshooting and documenting lab work

Core Topics

  • Ohm's/Kirchhoff's laws, series/parallel, RC/RL time constants
  • Diodes, basic transistors, op-amp fundamentals

Practice Plan

  • Timed build-and-measure labs (resistor ID, voltage dividers)
  • Organized formula sheet; daily short problem drills
  • Oscilloscope basics if available; interpret traces
