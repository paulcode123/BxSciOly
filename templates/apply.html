What to Expect Next

Begin Studying

Start studying your selected events immediately. Create a study plan and gather resources like textbooks, online materials, and practice exams. The earlier you begin, the better prepared you'll be for tryouts.

Prepare for Build Events

If you selected any build events, start gathering materials and creating prototype designs. Experiment with different approaches and track your results to demonstrate your dedication during tryouts.

Tryout Schedule

Tryouts will be held in October. You'll receive an email with specific dates and details approximately two weeks before tryouts begin. Be prepared to demonstrate your knowledge and skills for each event you selected.

Team Announcement

Team selections will be announced by early November. If selected, you'll be assigned to Team A, B, or C based on your performance, and will begin regular event practices immediately thereafter.
