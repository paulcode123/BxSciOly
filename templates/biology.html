Events from Science Olympiad

Biology events in Science Olympiad cover a wide range of topics from human anatomy to microbiology and ecology. These events challenge your knowledge of biological systems and processes while developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

2025 Biology Events

Anatomy and Physiology

Students will be assessed on their understanding of the anatomy and physiology of selected body systems, this year focusing on the respiratory, digestive, and immune systems.

Study Event Team of 2

Disease Detectives

This event requires students to apply principles of epidemiology to a disease outbreak investigation, studying the distribution and determinants of health-related states in specified populations.

Study Event Team of 2

Ecology

Students will demonstrate their understanding of ecological principles, population dynamics, community interactions, ecosystems, and human impacts on the environment.

Study Event Team of 2

Entomology

Students will demonstrate their knowledge of insect taxonomy, biology, ecology, and their interactions with humans and the environment.

Study Event Team of 2

Microbe Mission

Students will answer questions, solve problems, and analyze data pertaining to microbes - bacteria, viruses, fungi, protists, and other infectious agents.
